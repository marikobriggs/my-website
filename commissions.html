Hire me!

What I Offer

I specialize in character portraits, stylized figures, and custom artwork. Whether it’s fanart, original characters, or gifts, I’d love to bring your ideas to life!

Pricing

  • Bust / Portrait: $50
  • Half-body: $75
  • Full-body: $100+
  • Additional Character: +60%
  • Detailed Background: +$30–$50

All prices are in USD. Final price may vary depending on complexity. Payment is via PayPal. I’ll send a quote and invoice after we confirm your request.

How to Request

Email me at you@example.com with the subject "Commission Request" and include the following:

  • Your name / username
  • Type of commission (bust, full-body, etc.)
  • Character references (links or attachments)
  • Pose / mood / expression ideas
  • Background (if desired)
  • Any deadlines or notes

Terms of Service

  • Personal use only — no resale or NFTs.
  • I retain the right to post finished work unless requested otherwise.
  • Commissions may take 2–4 weeks depending on complexity.
  • Two rounds of revisions: sketch and final.
  • No refunds after final piece is delivered.
