about

Mariko Briggs is an artist based in Seattle, WA. She works across traditional and digital media — acrylic, watercolor, graphite, and digital illustration.

Inspired by Seattle's music scene, she's worked with local musicians on album and single covers, show posters, merch, and social media visuals. More recently this has expanded into instrument customization.

She also takes on custom card alterations for Magic: The Gathering and Pokémon, and is open to pretty much anything else — if you have an idea, she wants to hear it.

Her process is collaborative. She'll work with you every step of the way to figure out what you actually want and make it happen.
