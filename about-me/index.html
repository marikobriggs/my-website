About Me

Mariko Briggs is an experienced and versatile artist based in Seattle, WA. She works in both traditional and digital media, and specializes in acrylic, watercolor, and graphite illustration.

Inspired by the vibrance of Seattle’s music scene, Mariko has worked with local musicians to design album/single cover art, merch, and social media promo material. More recently, this has expanded to instrument customization.

Her commissions are versatile, and as a lifelong lover of fantasy art, she can create custom alterations of your favorite Magic the Gathering or Pokémon card.

Contact her through Instagram @koyuki.png to inquire about her services or a new art project that you have in mind. Her process is grounded in collaboration, and she will work with you every step of the way to design the perfect piece for your vision and needs.
